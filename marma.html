Marma Chikitsa

THE ART OF HEALING AND VITAL POINTS

Restoring the Prana

In Kalari tradition, the practitioner who knows how to strike must also know how to heal. Marma Chikitsa is the ancient science of manipulating the 107 vital points (Marmas) in the human body where nerves, muscles, and energy pathways intersect.

Using specialized medicated oils, herbal pastes, and deep-tissue massage techniques (Uzhichil), we restore the flow of Prana (life force energy), aligning the skeletal structure and clearing nervous system blockages.

  • Sports Injury Recovery: Treating sprains, muscle tears, and joint dislocations.
  • Chronic Pain Relief: Managing back pain, arthritis, and nerve compressions.
  • Kalari Uzhichil: A traditional full-body massage to enhance flexibility and blood flow.
